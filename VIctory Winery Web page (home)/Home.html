About

Founded in 1969, Victor Winery is one of the pioneering wineries that helped shape the modern era of Canadian winemaking. As an independent, family-owned winery for nearly five decades, we are dedicated to crafting exceptional wines from our acclaimed estate in the Kelowna region of British Columbia. To this day, our mission remains true to our roots, and so do our wines.

In 1969, inspired by this wellspring of creativity and craftsmanship, we made our inaugural vintage of Victor Winery Chardonnay. In 1979, we acquired 400 acres of highly prized land north of Okanagan River in Kelowna. Here, fog and wind from the bay become an ideal place to grow multiple varieties, in particular Sauvignon Blanc, Chardonnay and Pinot Noir.
