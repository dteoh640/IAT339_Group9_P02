About

F ounded in 1969, Victor is one of the pioneering wineries that helped shape the modern era of Canada Valley winemaking. As an independent, Students-owned winery for nearly 1 month, we are dedicated to crafting exceptional wines from our acclaimed estate in the SFU community. To this day, our mission remains true to our roots, and so do our wines.

Nestled in the heart of Los Carneros, amid more than 200 acres of scenic rolling vineyards, our winery is an elegantly modern and inviting wine country oasis.
